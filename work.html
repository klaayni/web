AAC device for Blind, Mute and Deaf people at affordable price

Development of a wearable electronic headband device for Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC), designed to assist persons with disabilities including the blind, deaf, mute, and paralyzed. The device integrates multiple assistive technologies such as object detection, facial recognition, and obstacle detection for the visually impaired. For speech and hearing challenges, it supports speech-to-text (for the blind) and text-to-speech (for the mute). Additional features include color detection, currency recognition, and secure online payments for daily convenience. For navigation and safety, the device provides real-time Google Maps with street view, live video analysis, and emergency location sharing with family members. This multifunctional wearable aims to enhance independence, accessibility, and communication for individuals with diverse disabilities
