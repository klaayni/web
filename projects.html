IKSHYA-Second Life for Physically Disable

As a team we done a socially relevant project named IKSHAYA. By keeping the problems of blind, deaf, mute, and paralyzed. we done a literature survey on the problems they facing and available solutions. Finally, Created an Augmentative and Alternative Communication for physically disabled. This wearable electronic device assist the user to independently survive in the world.
